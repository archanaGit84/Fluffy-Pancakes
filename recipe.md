Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon and salt in one bowl
In another bowl, mix milk,sugar,vanilla and lemon juice
Add to the first bowl after mixing.
Mix everything and fold as less as possible
Add whisked eggs and mix again to make the batter
Heat pan on medium heat. Add some butter on pan and spread the batter with the back of ladle
Let it cook and add blueberries or other toppings of your choice.
When pancakes have bubble on top turn them to the other side and let them cook till they turn light brown.
Voila! Your fluffy pancakes are ready.
